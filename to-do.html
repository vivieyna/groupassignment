Check out these must-do activities:

1. Savour the Beaches

🏖 Nipah Bay: A well-liked location for swimming, tanning, and watching the sunset.

🏖 Coral Beach: Well-known for its calm surroundings and crystal-clear seas, this beach is ideal for snorkeling.

🏖 Teluk Belanga Beach: Less crowded, ideal for a peaceful day by the sea.

2. Water Activities

🛶 Diving and Snorkelling: Explore the colorful underwater environment. You can hire equipment from nearby operators.

🛶 Kayaking: Take a kayak rental and cruise the coast at your leisure.

🛶 Banana Boat Rides and Jet Skiing: For a little thrill and adventure on the water.

3. Go to Historical Places

🚩 Dutch Fort (Kota Belanda): Tour the remains of this Dutch fort constructed in the seventeenth century.

🚩 Barang Bersurat : An old stone inscription from Dutch colonization.

4. Visit Temples

🛕 Fu Lin Kong Temple: Situated in a serene area, this Chinese temple has exquisite architecture.

🛕 Kali Amman Temple: One of the island's most important Hindu temples, providing insight into regional religious customs.

5. Natural and Animal Resources

💎 Forest Hiking: Explore the island's verdant forest paths. Trekking is familiar in Teluk Segadas Hill.

💎 Bird Watching: Pulau Pangkor is a fantastic location for avid bird watchers since it is home to many bird species.

💎 Hornbill Feeding: You may get up close and personal with these unusual birds at daily hornbill feeding sessions held by several resorts, particularly close to Nipah Bay.

6. Island Hopping

🎈 Pangkor Laut: Take a trip to this exclusive island renowned for its immaculate beaches and opulent resort.

🎈 Giam Island: A little island near Coral Beach perfect for underwater exploration and snorkeling.

7. Fishing

🐠 Take a Fishing Tour: Try your ability to catch local fish on fishing excursions provided by local companies.

🐠 Fishing from the Beach: Pack your gear or hire it out and fish from several locations along the shore.

8. Discover Local Culture

🔮 Pangkor Town: Stroll around the town, stop at the marketplaces, and sample some regional delicacies.

🔮 Factory Satay: Sample some fresh satay and tour a nearby satay plant to see how this well-known meal is prepared.

9. Unwind and Recharge

🎀 Wellness and Spa: Many resorts include spa services. Give yourself a spa treatment or massage.

🎀 Sunset Watching: Pulau Pangkor offers breathtaking sunsets. On the beaches along the west coast, choose a nice location to watch the sunset.

10. Shopping

💳 Souvenir Shops: In Pangkor Town and close to the main beaches, purchase handicrafts, batik, and souvenirs made locally.

💳 Dried Seafood: The dried seafood of Pulau Pangkor is a specialty. Stop by stores offering dried fish, squid, and other items for carry-out.
